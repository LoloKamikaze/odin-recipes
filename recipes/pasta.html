Pasta

Prepare 300g of uncooked pasta of any choice, a plate, a recipient in which you will boil your water, a pan, some oil. tomato sauce, some greens, and a fork to eat your pasta with

So start by boiling your water, when it's boiling, add your pasta in, while the pasta is cooking, add some oil to your pan and start heating it, when the oil is hot, add your pasta sauce, the greens of your choice, and cook it at a slow fire until the pasta is cooked enough, when it's cooked. rid the pasta of it's water and put the pasta in the pan with the sauce and greens, and stir for 2 minutes at a slow fire and voila, ready to serve
