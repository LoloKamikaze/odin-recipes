Pasta

What is pasta? Italian pasta (pronounced “PAS-tah”) is a collective name for a category of food made from wheat flour and water, sometimes with egg

Pasta is traditionally made from wheat, water, eggs, and salt. The first pasta dish was made in Sicily and dates back to 1154. Historians have noted that this first recipe was closer to a lasagna than to what we know today as our traditional noodles because it had a meat stuffing.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Greens of your choice
  • Any other spices you like

Steps

  1. Start by cooking your pasta
  2. Heat a pan with olive
  3. Stir fry your tomato sauce with the greens
  4. When the pasta is ready, rid of it of the water and drop the pasta in the pan
  5. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes at low heat the pan with the pasta and sauce
  6. Season with extra spices you like
  7. And serve, voila
