The nices eggs you will ever put in your mouth... wait... anyways, quick info: eggs are and old dish and you can literally cook them in any way you want, there are at least 150 ways you can use eggs in your dishes.

These are the tastiest eggs you will ever cook in your life, with a balanced diet between natural healthy fats, sodium and spices, you can literally make one of the best dishes on earth, which is.... frieds eggs, it's easy, convenient, and tasty, and here you will learn how to cook eggs, unironically, idk what else there is to say about fried eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • That's it
  • Lol, almost got you
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Any other spices
  • Bacon(optional)

Steps

  1. Heat a pan with the olive oil
  2. Crack the eggs and drop them in the pan
  3. Put some salt and spices
  4. Put some bacon if you want
  5. Put the lid on and let them fry for 5-8 minutes, depending on your personal tastes
  6. Serve and eat, Voila
